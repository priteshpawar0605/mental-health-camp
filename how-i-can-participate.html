How I Can Participate

Getting involved with MindSpace Teens is easy and meaningful. Follow these steps to join our supportive community and begin your journey toward sharing and healing:

  1. Click the "Register to Join" button on our Home page.
  2. Fill out our short Google Form to tell us a little about yourself.
  3. After submitting, you'll get the link to our private WhatsApp Community.
  4. Join the group and introduce yourself when you're ready.
  5. Share your story, listen to others, and help build a safe, supportive space for everyone.

We're excited to have you join us. Together, we can make mental wellness accessible and stigma-free for all teens!
